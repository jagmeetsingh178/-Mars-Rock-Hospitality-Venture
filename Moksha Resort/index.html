About Moksha

Escape to the height of luxury in the heart of Himachal

The Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, located in Parwanoo, offers a luxurious escape amidst the majestic Shivalik Ranges. With its 5000ft elevation, the resort is surrounded by lush green mountains, making it the ideal destination for those seeking both peace and adventure.

Moksha, a top luxury resort in Himachal Pradesh, is renowned for its Ayurvedic spa, cable car, infinity pool, stunning views, and culinary experiences. At Moksha, you can fully unwind and allow the serene atmosphere to revitalize your mind and spirit.

Please Note: The Cable Car can be boarded from the base at Timber Trail Resort from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
